About this site

I was gathering up useful resources for Slovenian CoderDojo course, and I stumbled upon light-Bot 2 and RoboZZle at HappyNerds' wonderful collection of programming resources for kids.

One game led to the other, and — after a few days of ecstatic playing — I figured I would make the job easier for others pursuing the same task – searching for games with real educational value that help one develop not only the interactive problem-solving skills, but also create very real hands-on experience or depth of understanding of a particular real-world field. And for watchful parents and their likes. Gathered are, incidently, some of the best puzzle games ever created.

With that regard, I have a challenge for you. Go search for other "educational games" online and compare your findings with the games listed here. You might find the state of affairs is quite sad, really.

Find the games in the menu above. Most games require Adobe Flash, some are links to their respective websites.

If you have a suggestion for an educative game or a new category, drop me a line on kerncece+edugames/on\gmail, or better yet, if you wish, just add the new game yourself via a "pull request" on GitHub. :)
